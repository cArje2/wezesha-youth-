Who we are

About Us

WEZESHA YOUTH INITIATIVE (KIBRA) is a Community Based Organisation (CBO) Wezesha youth initiative kibra is a community based organization committed to empowering youths across various aspects of life.It was founded with the goal of transforming lives,especially in underserved areas such as kibra.The initiative is rooted in equipping young people with tools,skills and support they need to achieve meaningful,self-sufficient futures. Its focus spans rehabilitation of child mothers and fathers, career guidance, skill-building and sourcing scholarships to ensure that no young person is left behind due to financial or social limitations.

promoting the holistic development of youth, ensuring equitable access to opportunities, and fostering their active participation in national development of kenya.

Our Mission

To transform the lives of vulnerable youth by rehabilitating and reintegrating teenage mothers into the education system, sourcing scholarships to ensure equal access to quality education for both boys and girls, providing holistic guidance and counseling services, and empowering young people with the skills, knowledge, and opportunities they need to build a brighter future." .

Our Vision

To build a future where every young person regardless of circumstance, has the opportunity to thrive through education, empowerment, and holistic support. We envision a society where teenage mothers are rehabilitated and empowered to reclaim their dreams through schooling, where both boys and girls access quality education through scholarships, and where guidance, counseling, and youth development initiatives inspire confident, capable, and responsible generations.

We believe access to education, resources and schorlarships that accrue from well wishers and sponsors as well as the government on youth empowerment and development plans is crucial for every youth both boys and girls to access educationn. We strongly seek for natural resources integrity to spur development of our communitty and country at large.

Our goal

Wezesha Youth Initiative Kenya was established with the following key objectives:

Career Empowerment: To provide youths with practical skills and knowledge that enable them to pursue various career paths with confidence and competence.

Support for Teenage Mothers: To rehabilitate and support teenage mothers, enabling them to return to school and continue their education.

Guidance & Counselling: To offer structured guidance and career counselling to help youths make informed life decisions.

Access to Education: To actively seek and secure scholarships for needy and deserving youths to access secondary and tertiary education.

Holistic Empowerment: To empower young people in all areas of life—social, emotional, educational, and economic—so they can become productive, responsible members of society.
